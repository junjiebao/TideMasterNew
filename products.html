December 30, 2024 Paint Completion

New Zealand's 2nd Ninja 18 Custom Paint Completed

We're excited to announce that the custom paint job for New Zealand's second Ninja 18 has been completed. The vessel will soon enter the outfitting phase, bringing this exceptional craft one step closer to delivery.

Ninja 18 Custom Paint Completion Details

The second Ninja 18 destined for New Zealand has completed its custom paint application, showcasing our commitment to delivering personalized vessels that meet each client's unique vision. The professionally applied finish not only enhances the boat's aesthetic appeal but also provides superior protection against harsh marine environments.

With the paint phase now complete, our team will proceed to the outfitting stage, where all systems, equipment, and finishing touches will be installed. This includes navigation electronics, fishing equipment, safety gear, and interior fittings tailored to the client's specifications.

The Ninja 18 continues to be one of our most popular models for international markets, particularly appreciated for its towability, fuel efficiency, and versatility in various fishing and diving conditions.

Hunter 28 Hull Completion Details
