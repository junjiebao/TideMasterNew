How quickly can you respond to an emergency service request?

We prioritize emergency service requests and typically respond within 4-24 hours depending on your location and support plan. Premium support customers receive priority response within 4 hours.

What maintenance schedule do you recommend for TideMaster equipment?

We recommend quarterly maintenance checks for systems in harsh environments and bi-annual checks for standard installations. Annual calibration is recommended for all systems to ensure measurement accuracy.

Do you offer remote diagnostics and troubleshooting?

Yes, our advanced systems support remote diagnostics, allowing our technicians to troubleshoot many issues without requiring an on-site visit, saving you time and reducing downtime.

How long does equipment installation typically take?

Installation time varies by system complexity. Basic systems can be installed and configured in 1-2 days, while more complex networks may require 3-5 days. We'll provide a specific timeline during your consultation.

Can I order spare parts directly from TideMaster?

Yes, you can order genuine spare parts directly from us. We maintain extensive inventory of common parts to ensure quick delivery. For specialized components, lead times may vary, but we prioritize parts orders to minimize your downtime.

Do you provide operator training for new equipment?

Yes, we offer comprehensive operator training as part of our installation package. Additional training sessions can be arranged on-site or through our online training platform. We also provide refresher courses and advanced training for experienced operators.
