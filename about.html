About TideMaster

Setting new standards in aluminum boat manufacturing

Who We Are

TideMaster was established to answer the specific demands of the Middle Eastern boating market. Since our founding in 2010, we have quickly risen to prominence by marrying innovative design and cutting-edge technology with time-honored craftsmanship in our state-of-the-art facility. The result is a fleet of premium aluminum vessels that delivers beyond expectation.

Our mission is to design and build aluminum boats that offer unparalleled performance, durability, and comfort. We understand the unique demands of marine environments in the Middle East and tailor our vessels to meet these specific requirements.

With a team of experienced naval architects, engineers, and craftsmen, we deliver boats that meet the highest standards of quality and performance.

Our Journey

The evolution of TideMaster Technology

2010

The Beginning

TideMaster was founded in city of Longkou, Shandong province China by the Chi family with a vision to create high-quality aluminum boats, tailored to meet global demand and diverse maritime conditions.

2013

First Commercial Fleet

Completed our first major commercial order - a fleet of 26 tourism vessels for a prominent tourism operation company.

2020

New Facility

Expanded into a state-of-the-art 35,000 sqm production facility in Longkou City, increasing our production capacity by 600%.

2022

Production Milestone Reached

We set a new annual record by manufacturing 150 boats in our new state-of-the-art facility this year.

2023

AI Integration

Revolutionized the regional boating industry with the introduction of our AI-integrated navigation and safty systems.

2024

Strategic Patrol Boat Export

Marking a significant milestone, we have designed, built, and exported four distinct patrol boat variants for deployment with the Malaysian Coast Guard.

2024

GCC Expansion

We have extended our reach into the GCC with operations in the UAE and new service centers on the horizon in Qatar and Oman.

2025

Present Day

Continuing to innovate with new models, expanded services, and our commitment to sustainable marine technology.
